About Spark

"Spark- a creative fire which allows a person to grow!"

This is what SRCS had in mind when they named the annual inter-school competition, Spark. Our vision from the very beginning has remained the same: to develop our students holistically and to discover their intrinsic creativity. Bringing this into action, it gives us immense pleasure to set forth our inter-school contest, Spark. Organised by Shri Ram Centennial School annually, the Sparks Contest is a wonderful opportunity for participants from all over India to rekindle their "Creative Spark" and to exhibit their talents.

What started in 2016, as an Inter-School contest with merely four events, Spark has now been extended to a national level competition with almost twenty events, catering to all age groups.

Spark is an enthusiastic contest that has been near to the hearts of every SRCian for a long period of time. The whole school descends into a cheerful environment on this day, as many schools visit our campus in the hope of bagging victories for various events. Everyone enjoys the frenzy of activity as they prepare for their various activities. This competition not only demonstrates the students' competitive spirits, but it also provides them with an opportunity to meet new people and make new friends.

The pandemic prevented Spark from taking place in the year 2020, but we are returning with a blazing fire to give you the time of your life. With that, we'd like to extend a warm welcome to everyone participating online in Spark 2021!

Language: English
Mode of submission:Zoom(live) (Link will be shared with the school that registers 3 days prior to the event.)

Rules and Regulations:
